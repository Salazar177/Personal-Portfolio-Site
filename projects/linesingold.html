This publication layout project was created in Briar Levit's Digital Page Design I course in Winter 2012. The Lines in Gold exhibition booklet would serve as a handheld guide to the exhibit in the Sakip Sabanci Museum in Istanbul. A graphical approach was taken for the project to attract guest in an exciting fashion while respecting the pieces on display at the exhibit.

This information design project was created in Kate Bingaman-Burt's Communication Design Studio IV course in Winter 2013. The Agency League of Musicians is a local musicians advocacy group that works to connect and empower musicians which was in need of a new brand strategy. This included several business deliverables, a website mock-up, as well as a blue sky business front for future expansion.

This publication layout project was created in Nicole Lavelle's Typography I course in Spring 2012. Every year PDX Pop Now! has a summer concert series where they celebrate summer in the city of Portland. Keep It Cool! would act as a fundraising event for PDX Pop Now! summer concerts inviting people to participate in this once a year gig with a special t-shirt invitation.

This event branding project was created in Lis Charman's Typography II course in Fall 2012. The NW Scifi Film Festival is a fictional Portland event held by the Northwest Film Center at the Hollywood Theatre. The NW Scifi Film Festival is centered around showing classic science-fiction films in the hopes of showing the history behind the science-fiction film genre.

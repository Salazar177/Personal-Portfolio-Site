This branding project was created in Kate Bingaman-Burt's Communication Design Studio IV course in Winter 2013. The Blue Bridge brand is based on creating an elegant jazz bar in a modernist approach that attracts people looking for a fun, relaxing night on the town while enjoying the expressiveness of jazz.

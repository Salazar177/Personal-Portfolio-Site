This information design project was created in Kate Bingaman-Burt's Communication Design Studio III course in Fall 2012. Pulse is a mockup for an interactive website for an exhibit at Seattle's Experience Music Project that would serve as an overview of the world of electronic music to those unaware of the influence electronic music holds in the music world. This infographic website was designed as a large scrolling website with parallax elements leading the audience through the most popular genres of electronic music as well as some of the history behind electronic music.
